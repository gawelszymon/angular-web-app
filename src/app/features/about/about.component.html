About Asian Cuisine Recipes

🌏 Our Mission

We believe in the power of food to bring people together. Our platform is dedicated to sharing authentic Asian cuisine recipes from various cultures and traditions. From the bustling streets of Bangkok to the traditional kitchens of Beijing, we celebrate the rich culinary heritage of Asia.

🍜 Why Asian Cuisine?

Asian cuisine represents thousands of years of culinary tradition, blending unique spices, cooking techniques, and fresh ingredients. Each recipe tells a story of culture, family, and tradition. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced chef, our recipes are designed to inspire and guide you on your culinary journey.

✨ What We Offer

  • Diverse Recipes: From Thai pad thai to Chinese dim sum, Japanese ramen to Indian curry, we have recipes from all major Asian cuisines.
  • Clear Instructions: Each recipe includes detailed step-by-step instructions with ingredient lists and cooking times.
  • Difficulty Levels: Whether you're a beginner or an experienced cook, find recipes that match your skill level.
  • Community Driven: Share your own recipes and join our growing community of food enthusiasts.

🛠️ Built With Modern Technology

This application showcases best practices in full-stack web development:

  • Frontend: Angular 17 - Modern, component-based framework
  • Styling: SCSS - Professional CSS preprocessing
  • Backend: FastAPI - Fast, type-safe Python API
  • Architecture: Feature-based modular design with lazy loading

📚 Angular Concepts Demonstrated

This project is an educational showcase of Angular's best practices:

  • Component-based architecture
  • Routing and lazy loading
  • Dependency Injection
  • Reactive Forms
  • Services and Observables
  • RxJS operators
  • Template binding and directives
  • Standalone components
